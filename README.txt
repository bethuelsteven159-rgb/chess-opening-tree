ROAD TO GM - OPENING TREE LAB

How to use:
1. Open index.html in your browser.
2. Click any move/branch in the tree.
3. Add your explanation, plans, traps, and model games.
4. Click Random Card to drill yourself.
5. Use Export to save your repertoire as JSON.
6. Use Import to restore or move your repertoire to another device.

Your notes are saved in the browser using localStorage. Export regularly so you never lose your work.

Suggested workflow:
- After every serious game, add one opening note.
- Add the common reply your opponent played.
- Write the plan in simple human words, not engine poetry.
- Drill 5 random cards before playing rapid/classical games.
